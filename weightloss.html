2 kg/week Weight-Loss Schedule + Height Guide

(No Walk to School)
Calorie burns → grams of fat loss; 1 kg fat ≈ 7,700 kcal

Time Activity Purpose
5:30 AM Wake up + black coffee/green tea Kickstart metabolism, suppress hunger
5:35 – 5:55 AM Indoor cardio (20 min)
• Jump rope OR
• Bodyweight HIIT (burpees, mountain climbers, high knees)		 Burn ~20–25 g fat
6:00 AM – 6:00 PM School hours Drink water only (2–3 L), zero snacks
6:05 – 7:05 PM Home workout (60 min)
• 20 push-ups
• 50 squats
• 30-sec plank (x3)
• 15 min jog in place / jump rope		 Burn ~50–65 g fat, preserve muscle
7:10 PM Shower + ONE MEAL
• 150 g grilled chicken
• 200 g steamed broccoli
• 50 g cooked rice (plain)		 ~600 kcal = ~78 g fat equivalent (fuel + deficit)
8:00 – 8:20 PM 20 min YouTube cardio (HIIT/dance) Burn ~25 g fat
10:00 PM Read or light stretch Wind down
12:00 AM Sleep (7+ hrs) Recovery + fat-burning hormones

Weekend Bonus

- Add 1 extra 30-min cardio session (Sat & Sun) → +35–40 g fat burn each
→ Total weekly fat burn: ~1,800–2,000 g = ~2 kg loss
